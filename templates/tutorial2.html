Common terms used in discussion


“Deadstock / DS” - The shoe is Brand New

“DSWT” - Deadstock, “With Tags”

“MINT” - The shoe is lightly worn and in perfect condition

“Beaters” - A shoe that has been heavily worn and this visually shows

“Grail” - A shoe that holds value to someone, usually they do not own it and are hoping to someday.

“Brick” - A shoe that has no resale value and tends to sit on shelves in stores.

“Retail” - The original price of the shoe

“GR” - “General Release”, referring to a shoe that is produced in mass amounts and is usually not difficult to acquire for retail price.

“OBO” - “Or best offer”

“BIN” - “Buy it now”

“Ask” - The asking price of a shoe, commonly used to refer to the “Buy Now” price on StockX

“SKU” - The numbered identifier of a shoe, every one is different

“Cop” - To acquire
