How to buy sneakers


Lots of online marketplaces have unauthentic sneakers floating around and they can be very hard to spot, so we encourage all of our users to be safe when shopping online.


Safe second hand distributors that allow you to purchase directly from other sneaker heads at competitive pricing include StockX, GOAT, and FlightClub



Please note that we do not encourage you to buy directly from other users of SoleTogether, as we cannot promise the integrity of all of our users.
